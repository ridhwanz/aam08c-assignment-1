THEMED ZONES:


HOLLYWOOD


The zone is filled with Hollywood-inspired architecture and palm trees, including a replica of the Hollywood Walk of Fame.It is themed after the real Hollywood Boulevard of the 1970s.

NEW YORK CITY


The zone features various landmarks commonly portrayed in movies including the city skyline, neon lights, facades, and sidewalks. Also includes a replica of the New York Public Library.

SCI-FI CITY


Sci-Fi City is themed to a supposed metropolis of the futurea ccording to popular science fiction franchises. The zone is home to the thrilling 'Battlestar Galatica' roller coasters.

ANCIENT EGYPT


Ancient Egypt is based on the historical adaptation of Ancient Egypt during the 1930s Golden Age of Egyptian Exploration. It features obelisks, pyramids & Pharaohs' tombs which are typical of Ancient Egypt.

LOST WORLD


The Lost World contains many traditional attractions present in most Universal Studios parks around the world and is divided into two sub areas: Jurassic Park and Waterworld.

FAR FAR AWAY


Themed after The Kingdom of Far Far Away featured in DreamWorks Animations' Shrek. The zone consists of many locations from the film series, with a landmark in the form of "Far Far Away Castle"

MADAGASCAR


The zone features a tropical jungle theme inspired by the DreamWorks Animation franchise of the same name.
